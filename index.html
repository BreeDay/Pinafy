Mission

DiagnosX offers a new approach to access to care and alleviating the burden on providers.

Physicians spend 17-24 minutes with patients

In the US physicians spend almost 25 minutes with each patient. This means that during an 8 hour work day, a physician could see only 15 patients with respects to time for documenting medical records, reviewing old records and performing procedures.

Shortage of up to 124,000 physicians in 12 years

According to the AAMC, the US is expected to see a mass shortage of physicians by 2034. During this time period, roughly 40% of physicians will have reached traditional retirement age.

Growth of population and disparities in care

Over the next 12 years, the population of the US is expected to grow more than 10%. As of 2019, 60 million Americans lived in rural areas with limited access to healthcare.

Benefits

Gives new meaning to quality of care.

DiagnosX offers a deep dive into patient's diagnosis.

While physicians may have about 25 minutes to spend with patients, DiagnosX is available 24/7 to fully break down medical terminology, lab tests, radiology reports, and results with patients.

DiagnosX uses 3D models to detail plan of care.

Patients will have access a detailed and interactive 3D model of their diagnosis for a visual understanding of their condition.

One way to assist with the physician shortage is to have them in multiple places at once.

DiagnosX becomes an extension of the physician, answering most of the typical questions patients may have for their doctor, allowing more time to focus on niche questions and other issues.
