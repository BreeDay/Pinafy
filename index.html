Turn your shopping cart into cash

Giving customers the option of making money from potential purchases.

Why

$260 Billion dollars worth of products are left in online shopping carts every year.

Solution

Give customers more options to escape the buy or save dichotomy.

Currently, users only have two options when shopping online.

User's only options when shopping online today are buying a product or saving their money.

Online shopping is often done on impluse.

Most online shoppers add items to their cart on impluse and rethink their decision to buy after seeing the amount of money they are spending. With record breaking inflation, more shoppers are opting to conserve their money.

Pinafy offers a third option which can make users money.

Pinafy allows customers to invest the money they would have otherwise spent on impluse or lose value due to inflation. Businesses benefit by having users buy their product, invest in their company or both.
